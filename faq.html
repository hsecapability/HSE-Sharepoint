Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find my Capability Manager ?

To find your Capability Manager, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to My HCLTech Portal.
  2. Navigate to Employee Self Service (ESS).
  3. Go to ESS Home (located on the top right corner).
  4. Under Organization Info section, you will find your capability manager info.
I Need some clarification / additional support regarding technical query . Where should i ask ?

Great! We have our own HSE community where you can post your clarifications, doubts, or any technical discussions. The respective people will approach you to provide assistance and support. You can access the community by clicking the Technical Discussion button on our HSE home page.

Are there any case studies available in HSE site and how can I access them?

Yes, there are case studies available on this website. To access them, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the home page of the website.
  2. Look for the Knowledge Repository icon and click on it.
  3. You will be redirected to a form.
  4. Provide your Name, SAP ID, Capability Unit (CU), and the reason for access.
  5. Once you have filled in all the required information, submit the form.
  6. After submission, you will receive access to the knowledge repository.

This process will grant you access to a wealth of case studies in HSE site

Are there recordings available for the trainings?

Yes, recordings of the trainings are available. To access them, you need to navigate to the training page specific to your Capability Unit (e.g., RACE - Training page). On this page, you will find a list of trainings that have been conducted. Look for the column named "Link," where the recorded session links are provided. By clicking on the desired link, you can access the recorded session of the training you are interested in. This process should help you easily access the training recordings.

I have a new ideas / Innovation solutions . whom should i contact to get it done ?

Nice! You can post your ideas on our HSE site by navigating to your Capability Unit (CU) - ACE page (e.g., SysD ACE). This will redirect you to a form where you can fill in the relevant details and submit it. After your submission, the CU Head or CM will approach you to discuss your idea further.

Do I need to change my Reporting Manager (RM) if I move to the bench?

No, your current RM will remain the same until you transition to a new opportunity or project.

Can my Capability Manager become my Reporting Manager?

Mostly no. However, in some cases, your Capability Manager might also serve as your Reporting Manager pertaining to geographical constraints.

How can I find out which Capability Unit (CU) I belong to?

You can find your CU in capitalized letters under your name at the end of the profile detail page. You may also refer to the image below for reference.

Where can CMs find the list of their associates?

There are two ways to find this information:

  1. On the HSE homepage under the CM Mapping section – simply select your name to view the list of your associates.
  2. In ESS Home, under the Reports section – look for the report titled List of Deployables to view your associates.
What if there is mismatch in list of associates with CM mapping and reports?

Kindly communicate with CU head to resolve the issue and ensure the data is updated accurately.

I wish to request for project rotation . whom should i approach?

Kindly reach out your CM .The CM will consult with your RM and take the request forward accordingly.
